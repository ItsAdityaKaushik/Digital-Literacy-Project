Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not respond to online harassment; block and report abusive users immediately.
- Keep social media accounts private and review privacy settings regularly.
- Avoid sharing personal information such as phone number, address, or passwords online.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Take screenshots as evidence if you face any online abuse or suspicious activity.
- Talk to a trusted person or authority if you feel uncomfortable or threatened online.

Financial / UPI Safety:
- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone.
- Always verify the UPI ID or QR code before making any payment.
- Avoid clicking on unknown payment links or requests.

Reporting:
- Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in.
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930.
